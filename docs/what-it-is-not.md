# What Aureum One Is / What It Is Not

## Aureum One **Is**

* Institutional infrastructure
* Governance-by-design system
* Coordination layer across independent counterparties
* Evidence-first architecture
* Read-only verifiable for auditors

---

## Aureum One **Is Not**

* ❌ an investment product
* ❌ a token issuer
* ❌ a custody solution
* ❌ a trading platform
* ❌ a marketplace
* ❌ a financial intermediary
* ❌ a yield-generating structure
* ❌ a substitute for custodians or banks

No exception exists to this list.
