# Audit Pack & Evidence Bundle Definition

## Purpose

This document defines **how auditors consume evidence**.

Auditors do not interview the system.
They **consume deterministic evidence bundles**.

---

## Audit Pack Characteristics

An audit pack:

* is generated deterministically
* contains only primary evidence
* is read-only
* is reproducible
* contains no interpretation

Each pack corresponds to a defined period or scope.

---

## What Audit Packs Do Not Contain

Audit packs never include:

* opinions
* conclusions
* certifications
* assurances

Any conclusion drawn is the **exclusive responsibility of the auditor**.
