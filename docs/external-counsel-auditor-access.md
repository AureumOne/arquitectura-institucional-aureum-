# External Counsel & Auditor Access Protocol

## Purpose

This document defines **how third parties access sensitive materials** without compromising neutrality.

---

## Access Rules

External counsel and auditors:

* receive read-only access
* are scope-limited
* cannot redistribute materials
* cannot modify records

Access is:

* time-bound (when applicable)
* purpose-bound
* revocable

---

## Non-Reliance Clause

External opinions:

* do not bind Aureum One
* do not replace evidence
* do not alter system truth

They represent **external interpretation only**.

---

> **Law defines limits.
> Architecture enforces them.
> Evidence survives them.**
