# Peer Review Entry

Technical and institutional reviews are welcome **only** in the following scope:

* architectural coherence
* boundary clarity
* governance sufficiency
* evidence integrity
* failure containment

Reviews should avoid:

* commercial suggestions
* growth strategies
* product ideas

Peer reviews are treated as **confidential** and **non-public** unless explicitly authorized.

---

> **Trust is not requested.
> It is structurally replaceable by verification.**
